Case Study 02

Building & scaling internship programs.

Designing, launching, and scaling internships in marketing and game development — recruiting, onboarding, and operations.

Problem

Need to build a talent pipeline and grow brand awareness.

Goal

Launch and scale internship programs that bring in top student talent while extending the brand's reach into key communities.

Actions

  • Designed program structure end-to-end using a flipped classroom learning model.
  • Built recruitment funnels through Handshake and LinkedIn job advertisements.
  • Managed operations and onboarding for every cohort.
  • Provided guidance and feedback on deliverables for marketing interns.
  • Planned all virtual events and professional development opportunities, including guest workshops and feedback sessions.

Results

By the numbers.

800+ applications across both programs with a >15% acceptance rate.
125+ interns scaled across Forbes 500 colleges.
85 Average Net Promoter Score from intern cohorts.

Bonus

  • Increased engagement on Atlas Arena's LinkedIn and Discord communities.
  • Hired six part-time employees directly from our intern pool.
