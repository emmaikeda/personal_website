Case Study 03

Designing an email lifecycle from scratch.

Developed a full-scale, production-ready email engine designed to automate the entire player journey.

Problem

Atlas Arena, an edtech gaming product, needed a scalable way to communicate value to players without manual intervention.

Goal

Design and ship a production-ready lifecycle program that automates value delivery across the full player journey, from discovery to advocacy.

Actions

  • Mapped a 50-point user journey, identifying key "felt value" moments for automated intervention.
  • Drafted tailored copy for the entire 50-email ecosystem.
  • Developed branded, mobile-responsive HTML templates using Claude Code and staged the full library within SendGrid for production.

Results

The lifecycle engine.

A six-stage player lifecycle covering 53 automated email touchpoints — each one triggered by a specific user behavior or moment in time.

01

Acquisition

8 emails · Convert interest into access
  • Newsletter signup
  • Waitlist signup
  • Club partnership application
  • Support request
  • Handshake / Marketing list
  • Market research survey submission
  • Club lead scraper
  • Abandoned signup recovery
02

Onboarding

9 emails · Get them in + remove friction
  • Account creation
  • T+1 day: Mission
  • T+2 days: Profile setup prompt
  • T+3 days: Play the game
  • T+4 days: Set learning goals
  • T+5 days: Milestones intro
  • T+6 days: Invite friends
  • T+7 days: 7-day checklist
  • T+8 days: No-play prompt
03

Activation

9 emails · Hit the first "felt value" moment
  • First session scheduled
  • First challenge played
  • First team joined
  • First friend invited
  • First milestone unlocked
  • Discord connected successfully
  • Nexus Rating unlocked
  • 2 weeks to competition
  • Post-challenge feedback
04

Retention

10 emails · Build habit + belonging
  • Challenge scheduled
  • Challenge in 2 hours
  • This Week in the Arena
  • Challenge complete recap
  • Weekly scheduled sessions
  • Monthly community highlights
  • 1 day post-competition wrap
  • Account anniversary
  • New feature announcement
  • Quarterly NPS / feedback request
05

Reactivation

8 emails · Re-engage drifted players
  • 7-day lapse
  • 10-day lapse
  • 14-day lapse
  • 18-day lapse
  • 21-day lapse
  • 30-day lapse
  • 45-day lapse + incentive
  • 60-day final win-back
06

Referral

9 emails · Turn players into advocates

  • 10 challenges + club affiliation
