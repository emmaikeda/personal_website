Community Update

On the Horizon

Here are the latest updates from Atlas Arena.

Atlas Arena was created as an experiment to challenge the skills the world is losing through immersive, team-based thinking games.

We learned a lot about the pain points, motivations, and anxieties driving the next generation. What surprised us most is that their focus wasn’t on entertainment or cinematics, but on how completing our challenges could improve their job prospects.

Our initial experiment is concluding as of June 2026.

What’s Next

Now, we’re shifting our focus to answer the question:

Can our games reliably give employers the signals they need to vet and train new talent?
