About

A curious mind at the intersection of research and growth.

Background

From psych lab to growth team.

Education UC Santa Barbara Psychological and Brain Sciences (B.S.)
Location San Francisco, CA

My interest in psychology began with cognitive research, where I studied how people process information, form implicit associations, and make decisions. From using SteamVR to assess spatial navigation abilities, to conducting qualitative surveys on support-seeking intentions, I became fascinated by the human brain.

Today, I apply that same curiosity to marketing by studying user insight, designing growth loops, and shaping stories that move audiences. I approach growth problems through a research lens, driving results grounded in behavioral data.

Why Growth

Why growth marketing.

I'm drawn to the intersection of experimentation, storytelling, and human behavior. I enjoy solving problems that require both analytical thinking and a deep understanding of people. The experiences I build don't just scale — they genuinely connect with the people they're designed for.

Focus Area

An interest in education.

I'm particularly passionate about improving educational outcomes for the next generation. I've designed research programs for high school students and launched marketing internships for college students. Working in classrooms and building learning products is where I feel the most energized.
