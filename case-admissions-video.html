Case Study 04

College program admissions video.

Ideating and producing an admissions video (concept, script, and talent) to drive program conversion for UC Santa Barbara's Transfer Edge summer program.

Program

Transfer Edge is an immersive six-week summer program designed to give incoming transfer students a comprehensive introduction to the rigorous academics and vibrant culture at UC Santa Barbara. The program serves as a bridge between prior educational experiences and the next chapter of a student's intellectual journey.

Problem

Limited program awareness and admissions. Cohort quotas were not met in previous years (2020–2023).

Goal

Increase program awareness, applications, and admissions. Fill the designated cohort of approximately 300 students.

Actions

  • Defined project objectives and timeline.
  • Created and proposed a concept to executive leadership.
  • Developed the video script and matched corresponding filming locations to each line.
  • Recruited "actors" from previous Transfer Edge cohorts and current UCSB students.

Results

By the numbers.

10K+ impressions across @ucsbsummer and @ucsbadmissions Instagram, plus UCSB Summer Sessions YouTube.
~300 students admitted — the cohort quota was reached for summer 2024.
3 official UCSB channels published the final video.
